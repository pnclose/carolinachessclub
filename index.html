About

Hello, we're the Carolina Chess Club! With interest in chess skyrocketing across the university, we've created a new website to encourage our fellow Tar Heels to enjoy the game of chess. Scroll down to learn more!

Chess During Covid

Prior to the pandemic, our club would meet on Mondays in Union Room 3201. Now, we are active online, where we host regular tournaments, some of these involving small cash prizes.

Inclusion

Although chess is a notoriously competitive game, our club thrives on the inclusion of members at all levels. This allows our club to be a great resource, whether you want to study the game of chess or simply have fun.

History

Founded in 2010, the Carolina Chess Club has seen many members from all walks of life. With currently 60 members, we are the most active chess club at UNC, boasting large tournaments and high stakes games.

Mission

This club is a place where chess players of all backgrounds can meet to discuss strategy, refine their chess skills, challenge each other, and enjoy the age-old game of chess. In addition, we promote the game of chess as a tool for expanding intellectual exploration.
